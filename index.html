About Sugga Mamaz

Founded in 2018, Sugga Mamaz Sweetz began as a passion project between two lifelong friends who shared a vision of bringing gourmet catering experiences to Cleveland's most discerning clientele. What started in a small kitchen has grown into one of the city's most sought-after luxury catering services.

Our mission is to create unforgettable culinary experiences that exceed expectations and bring people together. We believe in using only the finest ingredients, supporting local producers, and maintaining sustainable practices while delivering exceptional service that turns every event into a masterpiece.
